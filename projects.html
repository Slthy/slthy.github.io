Microplus-Lenex: Lenex encoder for swimming competitions

Microplus-Lenex Web scraper and Lenex encoder for swimming events hosted by Microplus s.r.l.
The scripts convert results from Microplus's website in an XML text file, following the Lenex 3.0 guidelines.

Fantanuoto.it: the most played swimming fantasy-game in Italy.

Fantanuoto.it is a closed-source fantasy-game for international swimming events where users can sign up to the website and create/join leagues to play with friends.
The project is in development, but small tests have been performed during the last international swimming competitions.
My contributions involved the scraping and formatting of data from various sourced (without APIs) and other back-end interfaces.

fnTrackingPros: User-friendly CLI tool to retrive, store and elaborate data from fortnite competitions

fnTrackingPros is a tool that tracks changes in the seasonal Fortnite's Power Ranking Leaderboard.
The scripts gives the users useful infos regarding the season trends.

touchy: a SvelteKit-based social network

Touchy is a SvelteKit-based social network with a sleek black and white interface. The goal for this new-born platform is to reward user's creativity through the engagement algorithm.
With the same importance, we also beleve that our user's privacy should always be protected: no adv-cookie is used in our systems.

Unpwn: as simple as secure

Unpwn is a lightweight iOS Password manager that helps to keep your accounts safe from being pwned. With a simple UI, Unpwn helps people to secure their accounts, creating passwords based on user's preferences.
Every user can:

  1. Decide to use lowercase characters in their passwords.
  2. Decide to use uppercase characters in their passwords.
  3. Decide to use numbers in their passwords.
  4. Decide to use special characters in their passwords.
  5. The password's length, from 1 to 64 characters.

In version 2.0, Unpwn can check if your own password has been part of data breaches through Have i been pwned?'s database.
